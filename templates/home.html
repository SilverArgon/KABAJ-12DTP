KABAJ is an anonymous textboard website with subject based boards that users can create posts in.

FAQ SECTION:

How do I work this?

The footer has the three avaliable boards based on subject where you can access all the posts related to that board.

There is also a button you can press for creating posts which you can then view on said boards.

Once you in the post creator, put in your post name and post text as well as the category of that post.

Can I edit/delete a post once I've made it?

No. You just have to sit there and acknowledge the jumble of text you've sent through onto this website. It stays here forever preserved, unless an Admin deletes it.

Can I respond to other people's posts?

No.Go cry about it

Is it fully anonymous?

No, you still need to sign up with an account to make posts, but people cannot see who created posts.

Would this anonymous ability be used for malicious purposes?

I'm aware that there is a chance for malicious vandals to do no good on this website. That's why there's Admin accounts. They can delete the no-no stuff.
